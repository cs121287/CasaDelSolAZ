Our Services

At Casa Del Sol AZ, we specialize in creating unforgettable events tailored to your unique vision. Our versatile venue and experienced team can accommodate a wide range of occasions from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations.

Weddings

Your dream wedding awaits at Casa Del Sol. Our picturesque venue offers the perfect backdrop for your special day, with flexible indoor and outdoor spaces to accommodate ceremonies and receptions of all sizes.

Corporate Events

Elevate your next corporate gathering in our sophisticated venue. From conferences and team-building events to holiday parties and product launches, we provide the perfect setting for your business needs.

Quinceañeras

Celebrate this important milestone in style. Our venue offers the perfect blend of elegance and functionality to create a memorable quinceañera celebration that honors tradition while creating new memories.

Special Celebrations

From anniversary parties and baby showers to retirement celebrations and family reunions, Casa Del Sol provides a stunning backdrop for all of life's special moments worth celebrating.

Additional Services

Catering

Our expert culinary team crafts customized menus featuring fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. From elegant plated dinners to casual buffets and cocktail receptions, we create dining experiences that delight.

Bar Service

Enhance your event with our professional bar services. Choose from premium open bars, signature cocktails, or champagne toasts. Our experienced bartenders ensure seamless service throughout your event.

Event Planning

Our dedicated event coordinators handle every detail, from vendor coordination to day-of execution. We work closely with you to bring your vision to life, ensuring a stress-free planning experience.

Décor & Design

Transform our versatile space with custom décor packages. Our design team creates personalized atmospheres through lighting, florals, table settings, and more to match your event theme and style.
