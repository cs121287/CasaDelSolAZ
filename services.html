Our Services

Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard venue rental includes the use of the main hall for 8 hours, outdoor ceremony space, tables and chairs for up to 150 guests, basic sound system, on-site parking, and a venue coordinator. Additional services and extended hours are available for an extra fee.

We offer premium in-house catering services with customizable menu options. You may also choose from our list of approved outside caterers for an additional fee. Outside catering not on our approved list may be subject to a higher fee and must meet our venue requirements.

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (March-June and September-November). Corporate events and smaller gatherings can often be accommodated with 3-6 months' notice, depending on availability.

We require a 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your date, with 50% due 6 months before the event and the remaining balance due 30 days prior. Cancellations made more than 6 months in advance receive a 50% refund of payments made beyond the deposit. Cancellations within 6 months are non-refundable but may be applied to a future date within one year.
