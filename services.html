Our Services

At Casa Del Sol AZ, we specialize in creating unforgettable events tailored to your unique vision. Our versatile venue and experienced team can accommodate a wide range of occasions from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations.

Weddings

Your dream wedding awaits at Casa Del Sol. Our picturesque venue offers the perfect backdrop for your special day with indoor and outdoor ceremony options, elegant reception spaces, and comprehensive wedding packages.

Quinceañeras

Celebrate this important milestone in true style. Our quinceañera packages include stunning decoration options, dance floor facilities, catering services, and coordination assistance to create an unforgettable celebration.

Baptisms & First Communions

Celebrate these sacred religious milestones in our serene setting. We provide elegant spaces for post-ceremony gatherings with customizable catering options, creating memorable experiences for family and guests.

Confirmation Parties

Honor this important religious milestone with a thoughtfully planned celebration. Our venue provides the perfect setting for family and friends to gather after the ceremony in a joyful, accommodating atmosphere.

Birthday Parties

From children's themed celebrations to milestone adult birthdays, our versatile spaces can be transformed to match your vision. We offer customizable packages for parties of all sizes and age groups.

Anniversaries

Celebrate years of love and commitment in our romantic venue. Whether it's your first anniversary or your fiftieth, we can help create a memorable event that honors your unique journey together.

Graduations

Commemorate academic achievements with a celebration that recognizes this important milestone. Our flexible spaces accommodate both intimate family gatherings and larger graduation parties.

Engagement Parties

Begin your wedding journey with an engagement celebration at our elegant venue. We offer intimate settings that provide the perfect backdrop for announcing your commitment and future plans.

Bridal Showers

Host a sophisticated bridal shower in our charming spaces. We offer customizable packages featuring elegant decor, delicious catering options, and attentive service to honor the bride-to-be.

Baby Showers

Welcome your little one with a joyful celebration. Our versatile spaces can be decorated to match any theme, with packages that include catering, seating arrangements, and special accommodations for guests.

Corporate Events

Elevate your business gatherings with our professional spaces. From conferences and meetings to team-building retreats and holiday parties, we provide the technology, catering, and ambiance for successful business events.

Memorial Services

Honor and celebrate the life of your loved one in a dignified and comforting environment. Our compassionate staff will assist with arrangements to create a meaningful tribute with respect and care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard venue rental includes the use of the main hall for 8 hours, outdoor ceremony space, tables and chairs for up to 150 guests, basic sound system, on-site parking, and a venue coordinator. Additional services and extended hours are available for an extra fee.

We offer premium in-house catering services with customizable menu options. You may also choose from our list of approved outside caterers for an additional fee. Outside catering not on our approved list may be subject to a higher fee and must meet our venue requirements.

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (March-June and September-November). Corporate events and smaller gatherings can often be accommodated with 3-6 months' notice, depending on availability.

We require a 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your date, with 50% due 6 months before the event and the remaining balance due 30 days prior. Cancellations made more than 6 months in advance receive a 50% refund of payments made beyond the deposit. Cancellations within 6 months are non-refundable but may be applied to a future date within one year.
