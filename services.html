Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking your event at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for weddings and events during peak season (March-June and October-November). However, we occasionally have dates available for more immediate bookings, so please inquire even if your timeframe is shorter.

Yes, we offer flexibility with vendors. We have a list of trusted preferred vendors who are familiar with our venue, but you're welcome to bring in outside vendors with prior approval. All vendors must provide proof of insurance and be licensed as required by law.

Our venue can comfortably accommodate events of various sizes. For our Main Hall, we typically recommend a minimum of 50 guests, while our more intimate spaces can accommodate smaller gatherings. There may be minimum spending requirements depending on the date and season.

We do not offer on-site accommodations, but we partner with several nearby hotels that provide special rates for Casa Del Sol events. Our team can help coordinate room blocks and transportation for your guests. The closest hotel is just 5 minutes away by car.

Our standard packages include venue rental, tables, chairs, basic linens, setup and cleanup, on-site event coordinator, sound system, and parking. We also offer premium packages with additional services like decor, lighting, upgraded furniture, and more. All packages can be customized to suit your specific needs and budget.

Yes, we offer in-house catering with a variety of menu options that can be customized to your event and dietary needs. From elegant plated dinners to casual buffets and cocktail receptions, our culinary team can create the perfect dining experience for your guests.
