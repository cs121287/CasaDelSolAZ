Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking your event at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for weddings and events during peak season (March-June and October-November). However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to inquire about your preferred date.

Yes, we offer flexibility with vendors. We have a list of trusted preferred vendors who are familiar with our venue, but you're welcome to bring in outside vendors with our approval. All vendors must provide proof of insurance and be properly licensed.

Our venue can comfortably accommodate events of various sizes. For our Main Hall, we typically recommend a minimum of 50 guests, while our more intimate spaces can accommodate smaller gatherings. The maximum capacity is 300 guests for a seated event.

We do not offer on-site accommodations, but we partner with several nearby hotels that provide special rates for Casa Del Sol events. Our team can help coordinate room blocks and transportation for your guests.
