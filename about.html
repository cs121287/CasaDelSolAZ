About Us

Arizona's Premier Event Venue

Casa Del Sol is more than just an event venue—it's a destination where memories are created and dreams come to life. Nestled in the beautiful landscape of Goodyear, Arizona, our venue offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and elegant sophistication.

Since opening our doors in 2020, we've hosted hundreds of weddings, corporate gatherings, and special celebrations, each one uniquely tailored to reflect the vision of our clients. Our dedicated team brings years of experience in event planning and hospitality to ensure every detail is executed flawlessly.

At Casa Del Sol, we believe in creating experiences that exceed expectations. From the moment you step onto our property until the last guest departs, you'll be embraced by the warmth and charm that defines our venue.

Our Story

The journey of Casa Del Sol began with a vision to create an event space that would showcase the natural beauty of Arizona while providing world-class amenities for celebrations of all kinds. Our founders, lifelong Arizona residents with a passion for hospitality, discovered this special property in 2018 and immediately recognized its potential.

After extensive renovations and thoughtful design work, Casa Del Sol opened in spring 2020, quickly becoming one of the region's most sought-after venues despite the challenges of its inaugural year. The name "Casa Del Sol" was inspired by the breathtaking sunsets visible from the property, which bathe the grounds in golden light each evening.

Today, our venue continues to evolve and improve while maintaining the welcoming atmosphere and exceptional service that have become our hallmark. We take pride in being part of the most significant moments in our clients' lives, creating a legacy of beautiful celebrations that will be remembered for generations.

Our Facilities

Grand Ballroom

Our spacious 5,000 sq ft ballroom features crystal chandeliers, adjustable lighting, and floor-to-ceiling windows with mountain views. Capacity: 300 seated.

Desert Gardens

Our landscaped outdoor space showcases native Arizona flora and offers a picturesque setting for ceremonies and cocktail hours. Capacity: 250 guests.

Sunset Terrace

This elegant covered patio provides breathtaking views of the mountains and desert landscape—perfect for smaller gatherings. Capacity: 100 guests.

Preparation Suites

Spacious bridal and groom suites offer comfortable spaces for wedding parties to prepare, with private bathrooms and lounging areas.
