About Casa Del Sol

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Casa Del Sol was born from a vision to create Arizona's premier event destination that harmonizes with the natural beauty of the desert landscape.

What began as a family estate has been transformed into a world-class venue that maintains its intimate charm while offering modern luxury and exceptional service.

For over a decade, we've hosted countless weddings, corporate retreats, and milestone celebrations, each uniquely tailored to reflect our clients' visions and dreams.

Our Venue Features

Main Hall

Our grand Main Hall features 5,000 square feet of elegant space with vaulted ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and floor-to-ceiling windows that frame breathtaking mountain views.

  • Capacity: 300 seated guests
  • Built-in sound system and lighting
  • Climate controlled for year-round comfort
  • Neutral décor to complement any event theme

Desert Garden

Our landscaped Desert Garden showcases native Arizona flora, creating a stunning natural backdrop for ceremonies and outdoor receptions.

  • Capacity: 250 guests
  • Paved ceremony area with mountain views
  • Evening lighting for magical sunset events
  • Shade structures available for daytime comfort

Sunset Terrace

Our elevated Sunset Terrace offers panoramic views of the Arizona landscape, perfect for cocktail hours or intimate gatherings.

  • Capacity: 100 standing guests
  • Built-in bar area
  • Fire features for evening ambiance
  • Covered sections for shade and weather protection

Bridal Suite & Groom's Lounge

Luxurious private spaces for wedding parties to prepare and relax before the celebration begins.

  • Private bathrooms and dressing areas
  • Salon-style stations in Bridal Suite
  • Comfortable seating and refreshment stations
  • Audio/visual systems to monitor event progress
