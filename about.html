About Us

Arizona's Premier Event Venue

Casa Del Sol is more than just an event venue—it's a destination where memories are created and dreams come to life. Nestled in the beautiful landscape of Goodyear, Arizona, our venue combines stunning natural beauty with elegant design and exceptional service.

Since opening our doors in 2020, we've hosted hundreds of weddings, corporate gatherings, and special celebrations, each one uniquely tailored to reflect the vision of our clients and exceed their expectations.

At Casa Del Sol, we believe in creating experiences that exceed expectations. From the moment you step onto our property until the last guest departs, you'll be embraced by the warm hospitality and attention to detail that defines our approach.

Our Story

The journey of Casa Del Sol began with a vision to create an event space that would showcase the natural beauty of Arizona while providing world-class amenities for celebrations of all kinds. Our founders, drawn to the spectacular mountain views and vibrant sunsets of Goodyear, knew this was the perfect location.

After extensive renovations and thoughtful design work, Casa Del Sol opened in spring 2020, quickly becoming one of the region's most sought-after venues despite the challenges of opening during an unprecedented time. Our resilience and dedication to our clients' safety and satisfaction has only strengthened our resolve to create exceptional events.

Today, our venue continues to evolve and improve while maintaining the welcoming atmosphere and exceptional service that have become our hallmark. We take pride in being part of our clients' most important moments and creating a backdrop for memories that will last a lifetime.

Our Facilities

Grand Ballroom

Our spacious 5,000 sq ft ballroom features crystal chandeliers, adjustable lighting, and floor-to-ceiling windows with mountain views. Capacity: 300 seated.

Desert Gardens

Our landscaped outdoor space showcases native Arizona flora and offers a picturesque setting for ceremonies and cocktail hours. Capacity: 250 guests.

Sunset Terrace

This elegant covered patio provides breathtaking views of the mountains and desert landscape—perfect for smaller gatherings. Capacity: 100 guests.

Preparation Suites

Spacious bridal and groom suites offer comfortable spaces for wedding parties to prepare, with private bathrooms and lounging areas.
