Frequently Asked Questions

What is the venue capacity?

Casa Del Sol can accommodate up to 300 guests for a seated event with a dance floor. For cocktail-style events, we can host up to 350 guests.

Do you provide in-house catering?

We work with preferred catering partners who offer a variety of menu options and price points. You can choose from our list of approved caterers, or bring in your own with approval and a kitchen usage fee.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable. Cancellations made 90+ days before the event date will not incur additional charges. Cancellations made 60-89 days before will be responsible for 50% of the total venue fee. Cancellations made less than 60 days before will be responsible for the full venue fee.

Is there a noise curfew?

Yes, amplified music must end by 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends to comply with local noise ordinances. The venue must be vacated by midnight.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary parking for up to 150 vehicles on-site. For larger events, we can arrange valet parking or shuttle services for an additional fee.

Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes, you can provide your own alcohol. However, it must be served by our licensed bartenders, and you'll need to obtain event insurance that includes liquor liability coverage.
